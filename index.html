Character Description

Chrisostomo Ibarra
He is a wealthy man who studied in Europe about 7 years before, and build a school after. He was also the mastermind of the revolution.

Maria Clara
She is a beutiful teenage girl who is the dautgeter of Kapitan Tiago and biological for Padre Damaso, she was raised by Tiya Isabel.

Elias
He is the Ibarra's boat driver who was accused of some crimes, he had a family who tragically passed away in in the novel, and was like a friend to Ibarra.

Bruno
He is the brother of Tarsilo whose father was tragiclly killed by a Civil Guard who plans to take revenge by the help of Lucas and Crisostomo Ibarra.

Tarsilo
He is the brother of Bruno who also wanted to avenge the death of their father against the Civil Guards.
